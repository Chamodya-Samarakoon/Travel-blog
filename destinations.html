My Travel Stories

Sigiriya, Sri Lanka

Known as the "Lion Rock," Sigiriya is an ancient fortress and one of the most iconic landmarks in Sri Lanka. It offers breathtaking views and fascinating history dating back to the 5th century.

Highlights & Activities

  • Climb to the top of Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  • Explore the ancient frescoes
  • Visit the surrounding water gardens

Paris, France

The city of love and lights, Paris is famous for its rich culture, cuisine, and art. From iconic landmarks to charming cafés, every corner has something to offer.

Highlights & Activities

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower
  • Explore the Louvre Museum
  • Stroll along the Seine River

Bali, Indonesia

Bali is a tropical paradise known for its beaches, temples, and vibrant culture. It’s the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.

Highlights & Activities

  • Relax on Kuta Beach
  • Discover Uluwatu Temple
  • Enjoy Balinese dance and cuisine
